# Javascript: Operators

### Arithmetics Operators:

* Additon: +
* Sub: -
* Mul: *
* Div: /
* Modulo: %

### Assignement Operators

* Simple: =

### Comparison Operators

#### Equals: ==
e.g: a == b

### Triple Equals: ===
e.g: a === b

#### Greater: >
e.g: a > b

#### Greater or Equal: >=
e.g: a >= b

#### Smaller: <
e.g: a < b

#### Smaller or Equal: <=
e.g: a <= b
